Spinal Fusion Surgery in Delhi

Spinal fusion is a surgical procedure designed to join two or more vertebrae in the spine into a single, solid bone. This surgery is often recommended for people experiencing severe back pain or instability due to conditions such as spondylosis. The goal of spinal fusion is to stabilize the spine, reduce pain, and improve the patient’s quality of life.

What Are the Common Conditions Treated by Spinal Fusion?

One critical condition treated by spinal fusion is spondylosis. Spondylosis refers to the degeneration of the spine due to ageing, which can lead to the wear and tear of spinal discs and the formation of bone spurs. This condition often results in chronic pain and stiffness.

Other conditions that may benefit from spinal fusion include:

  • Degenerative Disc Disease: The discs between the vertebrae lose their cushioning ability, causing pain and discomfort.
  • Spondylolisthesis: A condition where one vertebra slips over the one below it, leading to instability.
  • Spinal Fractures: Fractures due to trauma or osteoporosis can destabilize the spine.
  • Spinal Stenosis: Narrowing of the spinal canal that compresses the spinal cord or nerves.

Spinal fusion surgery addresses these issues by stabilizing the affected areas of the spine, alleviating pain, and improving function.

How Is Spinal Fusion Performed?

Spinal fusion surgery involves several key steps:

  • Preparation: To assess the condition of the spine, the patient undergoes preoperative tests, including imaging studies like X-rays or MRI scans.
  • Surgical Procedure: The surgeon removes the damaged disc or bone and places bone grafts or implants between the vertebrae. These grafts help the vertebrae fuse.
  • Stabilization: Metal screws, rods, or plates are often used to provide additional stability during the healing process.
  • Recovery: After the surgery, the patient follows a recovery plan that includes physical therapy and lifestyle adjustments to support the healing of the fused vertebrae.

Our team can offer advanced surgical techniques and personalized care plans to ensure the best possible outcomes.

What Are the Benefits of Spinal Fusion Surgery?

Spinal fusion surgery can provide several benefits, especially for individuals suffering from severe back pain and instability. Key benefits include:

  • Pain Relief: Spinal fusion reduces or eliminates the pain caused by movement between vertebrae by stabilizing the spine.
  • Improved Mobility: Many patients experience enhanced mobility and the ability to perform daily activities more comfortably after recovery.
  • Enhanced Stability: Spinal fusion helps to prevent further degeneration and instability in the affected area of the spine.
