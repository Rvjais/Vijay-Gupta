Specializations

Comprehensive Neurological Care

Advanced treatments for a wide range of neurological conditions using state-of-the-art technology and techniques.

Stroke Intervention

Emergency mechanical thrombectomy and advanced care for acute ischemic and hemorrhagic strokes with rapid response protocols.

Brain Tumor Surgery

Specialized microsurgical and minimally invasive procedures for complex brain tumors with precision navigation systems.

Aneurysm & AVM

Expert management of vascular malformations through endovascular coiling or microsurgical clipping techniques.

Spine Surgery

Comprehensive care for spinal disorders, from disc herniations to complex spinal instrumentation and tumor removal.

Carotid Stenting

Minimally invasive endovascular procedure to prevent stroke by treating narrowed carotid arteries.

Trauma Surgery

Critical care and surgical management for traumatic brain and spinal cord injuries available 24/7.
